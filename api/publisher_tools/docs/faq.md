# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What is Publisher Tools?**
A: Publisher Tools is a comprehensive ad monetization platform for website and app publishers. It provides ad network mediation, earnings tracking, fraud prevention, and payment processing.

**Q: How do I get started?**
A: Register a publisher account → Verify your site/app → Create ad units → Add ad tags → Start earning.

**Q: What content is prohibited?**
A: Adult content, gambling, hate speech, violence, illegal products, copyright infringement, and malware.

## Revenue & Payments

**Q: How is my revenue calculated?**
A: `Gross Revenue × Revenue Share % - IVT Deduction - Processing Fee - Tax = Net Payable`

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Standard publishers are paid monthly on the 15th. Premium is bi-monthly, Enterprise is weekly.

**Q: Why was my revenue deducted?**
A: Revenue deductions occur for Invalid Traffic (IVT). You can dispute deductions within 5 business days.

**Q: What is the minimum payout?**
A: bKash/Nagad: $5 | PayPal: $10 | Payoneer: $50 | Bank Transfer: $100

**Q: How do I reduce withholding tax?**
A: Submit a W-8BEN form if eligible for tax treaty benefits. Bangladesh publishers pay 10% (vs standard 30%).

## Technical

**Q: My ads aren't showing. What should I check?**
1. Ad unit status is "active" (not paused)
2. Site is verified and approved
3. Floor price isn't too high for your traffic
4. No active geo blocks for your visitors
5. Ad unit is in test mode — disable for production

**Q: What is eCPM?**
A: eCPM = Earnings per 1,000 impressions. `eCPM = (Total Revenue / Total Impressions) × 1000`

**Q: What is fill rate?**
A: Fill Rate = (Impressions / Ad Requests) × 100. Low fill rate means ads aren't serving for some requests.

**Q: How do I improve fill rate?**
- Add more ad networks to your waterfall
- Lower your floor price
- Check and remove restrictive geo/device targeting
- Ensure your ad unit sizes match standard IAB sizes

**Q: What is a waterfall?**
A: A waterfall is a prioritized list of ad networks. When an ad is requested, networks are called in order until one fills.

**Q: What is header bidding?**
A: All networks bid simultaneously (vs sequential waterfall). Typically increases eCPM by 20-40%.

## Fraud & IVT

**Q: My IVT rate is high. What should I do?**
1. Review traffic sources — avoid traffic exchanges
2. Block suspicious IPs
3. Enable frequency capping
4. Check referrer reports in analytics
5. Contact support if rate exceeds 20%

**Q: Can I dispute an IVT deduction?**
A: Yes. Go to Invoices → Dispute. Provide server logs, GA data, or traffic source reports. Submit within 5 business days.

**Q: What causes IVT?**
- Bot traffic from scrapers/crawlers
- Click farms
- VPN/proxy traffic
- SDK spoofing (apps)
- Incentivized traffic on non-rewarded units

## Account

**Q: How do I upgrade my tier?**
A: Tiers upgrade automatically based on monthly revenue. Contact support for Enterprise custom terms.

**Q: What does KYC verification require?**
A: Individual: NID card (front/back) + selfie. Business: Trade license + TIN + owner NID.

**Q: How long does account approval take?**
A: Standard: 2-5 business days. KYC review: 3-7 business days.

**Q: Can I have multiple sites/apps?**
A: Yes. Standard: 5 sites, 3 apps. Premium: 20 sites, 10 apps. Enterprise: Unlimited.
